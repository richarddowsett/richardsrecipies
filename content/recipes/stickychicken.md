# Sticky Sesame Chicken
*Credit: Nathan Anthony's **Bored of Lunch***

*A healthier, lighter way to enjoy a takeaway favourite, perfect with egg noodles and pak choi.*

### Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
| Calories |
| :--- |
| **509 kcal** |

---

**Serves:** 2  
**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 20 minutes  
**Features:** Air-fryer friendly | Freezable

### Ingredients

**Chicken & Coating**
* 3–4 tbsp cornflour
* 1 tsp garlic granules
* 1 tsp onion granules
* 1 tsp Chinese 5 spice
* 300g chicken breasts, cut into bite-size chunks
* 1 egg, beaten
* low-calorie oil spray

**Sauce**
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 3 tbsp honey
* 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
* 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
* ½ tsp Chinese 5 spice
* 2 tbsp sriracha
* 1 tbsp garlic purée
* 1 tbsp ginger purée

**To garnish**
* 1 tbsp white sesame seeds
* 2 spring onions, chopped
* 2 red chillies, finely diced

---

### Instructions

#### 1. Coat and Air-fry
Combine the cornflour and spices in a bowl. Coat the chicken in the beaten egg, then in the spiced cornflour mix. Spray well with the oil spray and air-fry for 14 minutes at 200°C.

#### 2. Prepare the Sauce
Mix together the sauce ingredients in a bowl.

#### 3. Glaze
When the chicken is cooked, pour the sauce over the chicken and air-fry for a further 2–3 minutes until the sauce thickens, or add the sauce to a small pan to warm over a low heat.

#### 4. Garnish and Serve
Once cooked, serve garnished with the sesame seeds, chopped spring onions and red chillies.
