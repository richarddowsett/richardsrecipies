### 🌮 Kid-Friendly Bean Tacos

| Metric | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Calories** | ~280 kcal per 2 tacos |
| **Protein** | 12g |
| **Fiber** | 9g |
| **Fat** | 8g |

---

### 🛒 Ingredients

**The Filling**
* **1 can (400g)** Black or Pinto beans (rinsed/drained)
* **1 cup** Frozen corn
* **1 tsp** Cumin
* **1/2 tsp** Smoked paprika
* **1/2 cup** Mild salsa
* **1/2 cup** Shredded cheddar cheese

**The Base & Toppings**
* **8** Mini flour tortillas (or hard shells)
* **1** Avocado (mashed)
* **1/2 cup** Greek yogurt
* **1 cup** Shredded lettuce

---

### 👩‍🍳 Instructions

1.  **Mash the Beans:** Place drained beans in a skillet over medium heat. Lightly mash roughly $1/4$ of the beans with a fork to create a "paste" that holds the filling together.
2.  **Season:** Add corn, cumin, paprika, and salsa. Stir and heat for 5 minutes.
3.  **The "Glue":** Stir in the cheese until melted. This acts as a binder so the filling doesn't fall out of the shell.
4.  **Assemble:** Spoon the mixture into warmed tortillas. Top with a dollop of yogurt, mashed avocado, and lettuce.

---

> **Tip:** If your kids prefer a "beef" look, use black beans; if they like a milder, creamier taste, go with pinto beans!
